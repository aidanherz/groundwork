Property research

Pulls live records from NYC's public ACRIS database — deeds, mortgages, and the names behind them.

Data comes directly from NYC Department of Finance (ACRIS) — the same records title companies use. Searches can take a few seconds.

Tracing an LLC owner

When a search finds an LLC owner, Groundwork automatically checks the NY State business registry and shows the filing date, entity type, and the address where legal papers are served — often the principal's office or home. Match it against the mailing address on the deed or mortgage below: when the two agree, you've likely found where the decision-maker gets mail. For dissolved or oddly-spelled entities, dig manually at the NY State business registry ↗.

Contact lookup (Tavily)

Connect a Tavily API key to let Groundwork search the public web for the people and contact details behind an LLC owner. It runs only when you click “Find owner contacts” on a property.

Your key is stored only in this browser, never in Groundwork's public code. Treat it like a saved password — anyone with access to this computer could read it. Tavily bills your account per search.
